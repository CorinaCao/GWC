SCHOOL EPIDEMIC

STATS

81% of current youth (2013-2014 survey) reported their primary reason for use to be the e-cig flavors (Food and Drug Administration - FDA).
The vaping epidemic has risen an astounding 78% (2017-2018) among high school students, who are a prime target of e-cig agencies. That is an 11.7%-20.8% increase (FDA).

WHY

With the decline of conventional cigarettes, a new form of nicotine-delivery is on the rise: E-cigarettes, otherwise known as “e-cigs” are a new vaping technology designed as an alternative to smoking. Offering over 7,700 alluring flavors, e-cigs are trending among the American youth.
Because e-cigs have only been marketed in the U.S. for 10 years, limited research has been conducted on these devices, and even less on flavoring chemicals.Therefore most consumers do not know the effects of these toxins on their respiratory system.

HOW TO STOP


As a teacher or fellow peer, you might relate to walking into a bathroom and seeing your peer juuling. High school students have been known to vape in school restrooms and classrooms, since the nicotine and flavoring e-liquids are highly addictive.
